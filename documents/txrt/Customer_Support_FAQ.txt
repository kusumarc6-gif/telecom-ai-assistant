Telecom Customer Support FAQ

1. How do I recharge my mobile plan?
You can recharge using the telecom mobile app, website, or authorized retailers.

2. How can I pay my broadband bill?
Pay online through the customer portal, UPI, net banking, or credit/debit card.

3. Why is my mobile network slow?
Network speed may be affected by signal strength, tower maintenance, weather conditions, or network congestion.

4. How do I register a complaint?
You can register a complaint through customer care, the mobile app, or the website.

5. What should I do if my SIM card is lost?
Contact customer support immediately to block the SIM and request a replacement.

6. How can I upgrade my plan?
Log in to your account and choose a new prepaid or postpaid plan.

7. What is the complaint resolution time?
Most complaints are resolved within 24 to 48 hours.

8. How can I check my bill?
You can view your billing history in the customer portal or mobile application.

9. How do I activate international roaming?
International roaming can be activated through the customer portal before travel.

10. How can I contact customer support?
Call the customer care number or use the live chat feature in the telecom application.
